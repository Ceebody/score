Admin Dashboard

The Dashboard is your central command center. It provides an overview of the school's performance and quick access to key features.

School Performance Stats

Figure 2: Admin Dashboard Overview

Key Features:

  • Stats Cards: View total students, teachers, parents, and reports at a glance.
  • School Performance: A dedicated section (Brown/Gold gradient) showing attendance and completion rates.
  • Quick Actions: Fast access to common tasks like "Add Student" or "Publish Reports".

Managing Students

Navigate to the Students page to view, search, and manage all student records.

Actions Available:

  • Search: Use the search bar to find students by name or ID.
  • Filter: Filter the list by Class (e.g., JHS 1, JHS 2).
  • Print: Click the "Print" button to generate a printable list of students.
  • View Profile: Click on any student card to view their full academic profile and history.

Managing Teachers

The Teachers page allows you to oversee the teaching staff.

  • View all registered teachers.
  • Assign classes and subjects to teachers.
  • Monitor teacher attendance and performance.

Generating Reports

The system automates the generation of term reports. Go to the Reports section to:

Note: Reports are generated based on the scores entered by teachers. Ensure all scores are submitted before generating reports.
  • Select Class: Choose the class you want to generate reports for.
  • Check Status: See which students have complete scores.
  • Print/Download: Generate PDF reports for individual students or the entire class.
